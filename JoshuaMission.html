LATAM AEROSPACE TECHNOLOGIES

Joshua Mission is a collaboration between Acrux Aerospace Technology and Mechanical Engineer Professor Rodrigo Cassineli ( UTFSM ). On December 15, Acrux is going to launch their FOG30k mkll rocket in a test flight. During this launch UTFSM will incorporate a data acquisition STM32 based board, with the objective of measuring key launch and environmental parameters such as; vibrations, acceleration, temperatura, humidity, CO2 concentration, and position.

This project is part of the plan of UTFSM to become leader of the development team of the second chilean satellite, a Cubesat based design.

My contribution to this work was the sensor calibration, and the coding of the data acquisition firmware, latest version here.

Currently we are testing the codes and preparing the board stiffness to resist the launch.
