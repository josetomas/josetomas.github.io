José Tomás Domínguez

Personal Information

Mechanical Engineer and designer. My research interest goes from autonomous robot development, biodegradable material production for circular economy, advanced manufacturing, product development and design process. Currently I work as Researcher and Lab Manager at the Fabrication and Advanced Manufacturing Fab Lab UTFSM. The lab is an applied research center with the goal of development of real world application projects for the technologies and knowledge developed by students and professors during their research. My work goes from research, design, manufacture, project management, hardware and firmware development, or mentoring students development; sometimes all at the same time.

My personal interests encompass development of technology for sustainable development, applied research, manufacture and automatization. But I also enjoy cycling, traveling, climbing, playing with my daughter Celeste and soon spacewalks (hope so).
