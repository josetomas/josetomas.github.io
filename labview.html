2 AXIS MAPPING DATA ACQUISITION

Development of a firmware that controlled a 2 axis CNC machine, extensible to 3 axis. Instead of an endefector, the machine has a sensor to map a physical value in a specific area. The MyRio board stores the position and value in order to be able to compare them after.

This firmware was developed in the year 2018, with the objective of testing different antennas to read radio frequency signals. These signals were measured to detect Partial Discharges (PD).

We developed a novel device that complimented the physiotherapist perception of the patients health with data. A key in the physiotherapist work is his own haptic perception, our device complimented this through a sensorized wearable glove. The goal was to augment the information specialist could get from touching their patients.
