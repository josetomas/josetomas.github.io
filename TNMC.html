NEW MATERIALS FOR A CIRCULAR ECONOMY

The New Materials Coop is a startup funded in the year 2019 by Paloma Gonzalez, Jose Antonio Gonzalez and myself. With the goal of developing new materials based on industrial waste or decaying industries. Currently our work has centered mostly in wood based materials, as it is one of Chile’s biggest industries.

This company is a spinoff of our research started by Paloma Gonzalez at MIT and continued together with Jose Tomas Dominguez in the intersection of BioMaterials and digital fabrication. Our main product is Woodpack, currently we are participating with this product in the semifinals of MIT incubator DesignX with a pitch presentation on December 5.

Projects that have been developed with The New Materials Coop:

  1. Woodpack

  2. Wood 3D Printer

  3. 3D Printed molds for moulded wood pulp.
