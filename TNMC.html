SUSTAINABLE BY DESIGN

The New Materials Coop is a startup funded in the year 2019 by Paloma Gonzalez, Jose Antonio Gonzalez and myself. This company as part of our research with BioPlastics with the goal of advancing Woodpack into industrial scale production. Currently we are participating in the semifinals of MIT incubator DesignX with a pitch presentation on December 5.

WoodPack, our main product, a biodegradable plastic material to mass produce and easily dispose of without harming our planet. Woodpack is challenging the whole plastic supply chain and its “shipment to landfill” life cycle. It meets all the industrial protective packaging film requirements, high strength and optimal outdoor performance, adding landfill disposability.
